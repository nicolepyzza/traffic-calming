Can I sign?

To be eligible to sign, you must have

1) a driveway on Terry Mill Road between Fayetteville and Glenwood or

2) you need to take Terry Mill Rd to get to your driveway.

You also must be the owner. If two owners are listed on the home, both must sign.

×

How can I help?

  • Take a look at our spreadsheet to see what neighbors haven't signed. Spread the word and contact Nicole Pyzza or Ashley Evans if they're interested in signing!
  • Lots of homes on our stretch of Terry Mill are owned by businesses or people renting their homes. We still need their signatures! If you know the true owner of a home, please feel free to reach out to them and let them know what's going on.
